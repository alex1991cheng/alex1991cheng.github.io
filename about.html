About Us

VALTECS - Pal for Wheels

Our Brand Story

Valtecs is built for your every journey on wheels.

For decades, we’ve served drivers and vehicle owners across North America with premium replacement parts, assemblies, accessories, performance upgrades and interior & exterior vehicle products. Our coverage includes cars, SUVs, heavy-duty trucks, motorcycles, RVs, trailers and all wheel-driven vehicles.

Every Valtecs part delivers consistent factory quality, precise direct-fit compatibility and long-lasting durability. All products are engineered specifically to match North American road conditions, climates and original OEM vehicle specifications, keeping your vehicle safe and reliable mile after mile.

Whether you’re commuting daily, cruising highways, exploring backcountry roads, hauling heavy loads or traveling cross-country in your RV, Valtecs stands beside you for every drive.

"Your trusted partner on every road, Pal for Wheels."

Our Brand Heritage

EST. 1993

Our Origins

Our automotive legacy dates back to 1993, founded by CHEN X.L. as Haowang Automotive Instrument Factory. We are located in Tangxia, Ruian, a globally renowned hub for automotive and motorcycle component manufacturing.

Early production focused on precision dashboard gauges and instrument clusters for farm utility vehicles, building a long-standing reputation for accurate engineering and strict quality control.

Image Placeholder: Our Origins
21st Century

Generational Succession

Entering the 21st century, the family business was passed down to CHEN X.L.’s son, CHEN B. Under his leadership, we expanded into premium windshield wiper blades that fit all major global vehicle platforms, including American, Japanese and German models.

Later, CHEN B.’s cousins ZHENG Z.Q. and LI R.Q. joined the family operation, strengthening our core capabilities.

Image Placeholder: Succession
New Era

Brand Establishment

Together, the family team upgraded our traditional manufacturing model into a fully integrated business combining independent R&D and mass production. It was at this pivotal stage that Valtecs was officially launched.

The name Valtecs is derived from Value Technology, representing our commitment to harnessing the full power of technology and ongoing research & development.

Taking full advantage of Tangxia’s complete, world-class automotive supply chain ecosystem, Valtecs has grown into a full-range supplier of automotive, motorcycle and RV components.

Valtecs Today

Backed by decades of manufacturing expertise, rigorous product testing and extensive global vehicle compatibility experience, Valtecs has become a reliable choice for drivers across North America.

We maintain independent in-house R&D, stable production capacity and consistent global parts supply.
