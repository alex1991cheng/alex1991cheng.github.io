Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I purchase Valtecs products?
Valtecs automotive parts are exclusively sold through our official Amazon store. You can easily access our product listings by clicking any "Shop on Amazon" or "Buy on Amazon" button directly on our website.
How do I check if a part is compatible with my vehicle?
On each product page, we provide detailed fitment information, including compatible vehicle years, makes, models, and applicable trim/engine types. You can also cross-reference your vehicle's specifications with the product description to confirm suitability before ordering.
Are installation instructions included?
Most Valtecs products come with basic installation guidelines in the package. For additional support, you can also refer to product images and descriptions on our Amazon listing.
How long will it take to receive my order?
All orders are fulfilled and shipped by Amazon, so delivery times follow Amazon's standard shipping schedule. You can view estimated delivery dates at checkout and track your package directly through your Amazon account.
How can I contact Valtecs customer support?
For product questions, fitment help, or other assistance, please visit the Contact page on our website to reach our support team. We aim to respond to all inquiries promptly and help resolve your concerns efficiently.
