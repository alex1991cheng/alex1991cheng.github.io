Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy Valtecs products?
Currently, Valtecs products are exclusively available through our official Amazon storefront. Click any "Shop on Amazon" or "Buy on Amazon" button on our website to be directed to the correct product listing.
How do I know if a part fits my vehicle?
We recommend using Amazon's "Confirmed Fit" garage tool on our product pages. Simply enter your vehicle's year, make, and model to ensure compatibility before purchasing.
What is your warranty policy?
Valtecs offers a limited lifetime warranty on most of our premium parts against manufacturing defects. Normal wear and tear is excluded. Please refer to the specific product details or contact our support team for exact warranty terms.
How can I process a return?
Since all purchases are fulfilled through Amazon, returns are handled directly through your Amazon account within their standard 30-day return window. If you experience an issue after this period covered by our warranty, please contact us directly via the Contact page.
